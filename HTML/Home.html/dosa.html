How to Make Dosa

A crispy and delicious South Indian delicacy.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 6 hours.
  2. Grind the soaked ingredients into a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  3. Transfer the batter to a large bowl and let it ferment overnight.
  4. Add salt to the fermented batter and mix well.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease it with oil.
  6. Pour a ladle of batter onto the pan and spread it in a circular motion.
  7. Cook the dosa until golden brown and crisp. Flip if needed.
  8. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.

Enjoy your homemade dosa!
