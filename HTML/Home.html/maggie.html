Easy Maggie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Maggie noodles
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • 1 small chopped onion (optional)
  • 1 small chopped tomato (optional)
  • Maggie Masala (comes with the packet)
  • Vegetables of your choice (peas, carrots, capsicum)

Instructions

  1. Heat 1.5 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onions, tomatoes, and vegetables. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the Maggie noodles.
  4. Sprinkle the Maggie Masala.
  5. Cook for 2 minutes while stirring occasionally.
  6. Once the noodles are cooked and the water is absorbed, turn off the heat.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggie!
