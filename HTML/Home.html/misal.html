Misal Recipe


Ingredients

  • 1 cup sprouted moth beans (matki)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 2 teaspoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water
  • Farsan (for topping)
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Chopped onion (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges
  • Pav (bread rolls)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add the sprouted moth beans and mix well.
  5. Pour in water, add salt, and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Once cooked, serve hot with pav, topped with farsan, chopped onions, coriander, and a squeeze of lemon.
