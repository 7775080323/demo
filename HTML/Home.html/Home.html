Paneer Tikka Masala

Paneer Tikka Masala is a popular Indian dish made with marinated paneer cubes cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based gravy. Perfect with naan or rice!

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 2 large tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, gram flour, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Add paneer cubes and marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan and cook the marinated paneer cubes until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, heat another 1 tbsp oil and saut√© chopped onions until translucent.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  5. Add tomato puree, salt, and remaining spices. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates.
  6. Add the cooked paneer cubes and mix well.
  7. Pour in the heavy cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
